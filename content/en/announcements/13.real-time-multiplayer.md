### 🎮 Play Against Friends

You can now challenge a friend to a real-time Koi-Koi match! From the title screen, choose **New Match** under the Multiplayer section to get started.

**How it works:**
- **Create a game** — generate an invite code and share it with your opponent
- **Join a game** — enter your friend's code to jump in
- The match begins as soon as both players are connected

### 💾 Auto-Save & Resume

Life happens. If you need to step away mid-match, your game is automatically saved. Both players can return and resume right where they left off — no progress lost.

### 🏳️ Forfeit Option

Need to end a game early? You can forfeit at any time from the in-game menu. Fair warning: it counts as a loss. 😅

### ⚙️ Match Rules

When creating a game, you can configure the rules for your match — game length, yaku variations, and more. Your opponent can review the rules before joining.

Sign in to unlock multiplayer and start playing! 🌸
